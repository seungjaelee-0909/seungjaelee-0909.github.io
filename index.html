      About Me

      I’m an M.S. student at the IRIS Lab, Chung-Ang University, specializing in Image/3D Engineering under the mentorship of Prof. Hak Gu Kim. Before joining Chung-Ang University, I received my B.S. degree in the School of Electrical Engineering at Myongji University, Yongin, Korea, in 2024.

      My research interests include 3D/Image and Video Processing, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning, with a particular focus on 3D Reconstruction, Pose Estimation, and Multimodal learning.
